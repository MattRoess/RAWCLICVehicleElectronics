
# Corrections and New Sources for BEV Auxiliary Motors Report

This document outlines the necessary corrections to the "Comprehensive Analysis of Auxiliary Electric Motors in European and Asian Battery Electric Vehicles (2024-2026 Models)" report. The primary correction is the proper distinction and allocation of medium stepper motors as a separate category from medium DC motors.

## 1. Medium Motor Distribution Correction

Based on user-provided information, the distribution of medium-sized auxiliary motors (50-500W) should be corrected to the following:

*   **Medium DC Motors (50-500W):** Approximately 80% of the medium motor category.
*   **Medium Stepper Motors (50-200W):** Approximately 20% of the medium motor category.

This 80/20 split will be applied to recalculate the motor counts across all vehicle segments.

## 2. Verified Applications for Medium Stepper Motors

Based on new research, the following applications are verified to predominantly use medium stepper motors due to their need for precise positional control:

*   **Active Grille Shutters:** Precise control of shutter vanes for aerodynamics.
*   **Headlight Leveling Systems:** Accurate and rapid adjustment of headlight aim.
*   **Advanced HVAC Blend Door Actuators:** Precise control of air temperature and distribution.
*   **Charging Port Door Actuators:** Secure and reliable locking/unlocking of the charge port.
